How to export CSV from Sprinklr
Follow these steps in your Sprinklr Monthly Report to get the Top Posts CSV file.
1
Open your Monthly Report in Sprinklr and navigate to the Top Posts – YTM or Top Posts – MOM widget.
2
Click the ⋯ (three-dot menu) icon in the top-right corner of the widget.
3
Select Export Widget from the dropdown menu.
4
Choose Export as CSV. The file will download automatically. Repeat for each platform (FB / IG / Threads).
